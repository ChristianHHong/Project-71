School Church

Was established in 1978

Our school church is an Evangelical Baptist multi-site megachurch, based in Lake Forest, California. It is one of the largest churches in California, and is among the largest in the United States of America. The church has several campuses in California and around the world. Weekly church attendance was 32,500 people in 2022.
